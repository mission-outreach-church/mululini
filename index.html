Welcome to Our Community

We are a vibrant and welcoming church located at Kitui, Mwingi West, approximately 200 metres from Mululini Market. Whether you're new to faith, looking for a church home, or simply curious, we invite you to experience our services and connect with us.

Our mission is to foster a spiritual environment where everyone feels loved, supported, and inspired to grow in their faith. Mission Outreach Church is faithfully led by Reverend Jacob Musyoka and Grace Syuki.

Service Times

  • Sunday Worship Service: 10:30 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Sunday School / Children's Ministry: 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM (Concurrent with Worship)
  • Friday Kesha (Night Vigil): Every first Friday of the month (Check calendar for time)

Come as you are! We look forward to worshipping with you.
